  • Witchwoman's Human Soup

  • We have partnered with The WitchesAintBitches Initiative (TWI) in order to raise awareness for the rise in witchophobia over the past 400 years. 90% of proceeds go towards aiding witches during their humanitarian crisis. Amen.

    Ingredients

    1. Any human body part you don't mind eating
    2. Waffle border cauldron
    3. Gigantic waffle spoon
    4. Any spices, seasonings, or types of wood
    5. A friend :3

    Preparation

    1. Fill cauldron until the etched in mark, with water
    2. Heat up your conductive waffle bordered cauldron until the water is boiling
    3. (Optional) Sloppily make out with your friend while waiting
    4. Put in all human remains (or living parts, judgement free zone) and spices/seasonings/wood you brought (acacia wood tastes really good with paprika btw!)
    5. Stir the pot for about 30 minutes straight, no rest, with your gigantic waffle spoon
    6. (Optional) Throw your friend into the pot and cook them too
    7. Once it has been stirred, make serving sizes for you and your friend (if they aren't in the soup by now)
    8. (Optional) Eat the spoon and cauldron whenever desired
    9. Enjoy!! (The WitchesAintBitches Initiative thank you for your contribution! Amen)
