  • Fried Head on a Swivel

  • Deep web browsing might have your head on a swivel, but deep fried heads on swivels is one thing that WON'T get you arrested.

    Ingredients

    1. Some poor soul's cranium
    2. Bread crumbs
    3. Seasoning (Don't be THAT cannibal!)
    4. Metal swivel (Crunchy or melted creamy)
    5. An attitude of gratitude

    Preparation

    1. Get a human head somehow (judgement free zone)
    2. Stick swivel in the back of the head as picture shows (Stick it like a pig!)
    3. rev up those fryers (like that one spongebob meme)
    4. Marinate and season head
    5. Put bread crumbs on head
    6. Fry it!!!
    7. Enjoy!! (maybe with a side of pickled persons feet?)
