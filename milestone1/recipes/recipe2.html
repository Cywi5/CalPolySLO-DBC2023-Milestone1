  • Pickled Person's Feet

  • Pickled pigs foot, except WAY better!

    Ingredients

    1. Multiple human feet (healthier feet is worth the money, trust me.)
    2. Dell pickles (the big ones)
    3. Olive Oil
    4. Love!

    Preparation

    1. Get your feet (judgement free zone)
    2. Heat up stove for a while until hot
    3. Pour olive oil on your pan (reduces stick)
    4. Put in all of your human feet at once (except your own)
    5. Put about 1 pickle per foot, into the pan
    6. Cook pickles and feet in the hot stove, flipping sides occasionally, until cooked fully
    7. Enjoy!! (maybe with some fried head on a swivel?)
